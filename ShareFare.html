What is ShareFare?

ShareFare is a transportation app on your phone used for navigating nearby customer/s who also want to go to the same(or nearby) locations in order to share a taxi ride. To people who would like to share their taxi rides, ShareFare is an application that you can find your ride partner and split the price.

How does it work?

ShareFare is a free application available both in ios & android! You can easily download the app, allow it to access your location and start using the benefits of paying less, staying safe!

Why ShareFare?

Instead of just being a channel of taxi rides we also provide sharing the ride with others, more resources but less cost for our customers!
